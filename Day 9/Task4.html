Pushpa

Discription:

The Rise is a 2021 Telugu movie about a laborer named Pushparaj (Allu Arjun) who works in the illegal red sandalwood trade in the Tirupati forests. Pushpa is denied a surname because he is illegitimate and is constantly disrespected by his stepbrothers and society. However, Pushpa has a special power to smuggle red sanders, which earns him the favor of a local smuggler. Pushpa rises through the ranks of the red sandalwood smuggling syndicate, making powerful enemies in the process.
