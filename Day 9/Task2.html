Sita Ramam

Discription:

Sita Ramam is a 2022 movie about an orphan soldier, Lieutenant Ram (Dulquer Salmaan), who receives anonymous love letters from Sita Mahalakshmi (Mrunal Thakur) and sets out to find her and propose. The movie also features Ansari, a Pakistani terrorist who plans to separate the bond between Muslims and Hindus in Kashmir, and Afreen, a Pakistani student president at a university in London who is tasked with delivering a letter to Sita
